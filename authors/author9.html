Kōhei Horikoshi

Famous Work: My Hero Academia

Superheroes Action School Life Modern Shōnen Emotional Storytelling

"Anyone can be a hero with courage in their heart."

Kōhei Horikoshi is a Japanese manga artist born on November 20, 1986 in Aichi Prefecture. He is the creator of My Hero Academia, a superhero-themed manga that became globally successful. The series is praised for emotional storytelling and dynamic action scenes.
