Boichi

Famous Work: Dr. Stone

Science Fiction Adventure Educational Themes Detailed Artwork Seinen & Shōnen Works

"Science and art together can rebuild the world."

Boichi is a South Korean-born manga artist whose real name is Mujik Park, born on January 29, 1973 in Seoul, South Korea. He is famous for his detailed art style and dramatic storytelling. He illustrated Dr. Stone, a science-adventure manga that became popular worldwide for mixing education and entertainment.
