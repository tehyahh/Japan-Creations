Tite Kubo

Famous Work: Bleach

Supernatural Sword Battles Action Stylish Art Big Three Era

"Style and power together create unforgettable heroes."

Tite Kubo, born June 26, 1977 in Hiroshima Prefecture, is a Japanese manga artist best known for creating Bleach. The series became one of the “Big Three” manga of the 2000s and sold over 130 million copies worldwide. Kubo is admired for his stylish character designs and unique artistic style.
