Masashi Kishimoto

Famous Work: Naruto

Ninja Action Adventure Shōnen Iconic Characters

"True strength comes from bonds that never break."

Masashi Kishimoto, born November 8, 1974 in Okayama, Japan, is a highly influential manga artist best known for creating Naruto. The series debuted in 1999 and became one of the most successful manga franchises worldwide, inspiring anime series, movies, video games, and the sequel Boruto.
