Eiichiro Oda

Famous Work: One Piece

Adventure Fantasy Shōnen Award Winning Legendary Creator
"Adventure has no limits when imagination leads the way."

Eiichiro Oda is a Japanese manga artist born on January 1, 1975 in Kumamoto Prefecture, Japan. He is best known for creating One Piece, the best-selling comic series in history with over 600 million copies sold worldwide. The series became a global phenomenon and inspired anime, films, games, and live-action adaptations. Oda has won major industry awards.
