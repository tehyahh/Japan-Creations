Hayao Miyazaki

Famous Work: Spirited Away, My Neighbor Totoro, Princess Mononoke

Fantasy Adventure Drama Environmental Themes Animation Legend

"Animation can make the impossible feel beautifully real."

Hayao Miyazaki is a legendary Japanese animator and co-founder of Studio Ghibli. Born on January 5, 1941, he is considered one of the greatest animation directors in history. His films are famous for beautiful visuals, deep emotions, and magical fantasy worlds loved by audiences of all ages.
