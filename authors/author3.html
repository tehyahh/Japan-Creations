Hajime Isayama

Famous Work: Attack on Titan

Dark Fantasy Psychological Action Thriller Complex Storytelling

"Freedom is worth fighting for, no matter the cost."

Hajime Isayama is a Japanese manga artist born on August 29, 1986. He is the creator of Attack on Titan, one of the most popular dark fantasy series in the world. The story became a global hit for its intense action, emotional story, and shocking plot twists. The series inspired anime adaptations, movies, and video games.
