Music Drama Idols Coming of Age

"Find your true feelings through the power of music."

A group of teenagers in Tokyo are dealing with normal high school problems, stress, and trying to follow their dreams. They find a mysterious song on their phones that transports them to a magical, virtual world called 'Sekai.' There, they meet famous virtual singers like Hatsune Miku, who help them face their real-life problems through the power of music and friendship.
