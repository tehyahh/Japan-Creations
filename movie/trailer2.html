Princess Mononoke

Director: Hayao Miyazaki | Studio: Studio Ghibli

Action Fantasy Mythology Award Winning

"Live to protect the forest."

A young prince named Ashitaka gets cursed by a demon and has to travel far from home to find a cure. On his journey, he gets caught in a huge war. On one side are humans who want to cut down the forest to build their town. On the other side are the giant animal gods of the forest, led by San—a tough girl raised by wolves who wants to protect nature.
