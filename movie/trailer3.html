Howl's Moving Castle

Director: Hayao Miyazaki | Studio: Studio Ghibli

Romance Magic Adventure Fantasy

"A heart is a heavy burden."

Sophie, a quiet girl working in a hat shop, is cursed by a jealous witch and transformed into an old woman. Ashamed and unsure of what to do, she leaves her home and finds refuge in a mysterious moving castle owned by the wizard Howl. Alongside a fire demon and other magical companions, Sophie seeks to break the curse while a war unfolds around them.
