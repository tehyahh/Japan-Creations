Your Name

Director: Makoto Shinkai | Studio: CoMix Wave Films

Romance Sci-Fi Drama Global Hit

"I'm always searching for something, a person, or a place."

Mitsuha is a girl in a quiet country town, and Taki is a boy living in busy Tokyo. One day, they start magically swapping bodies when they go to sleep! They have to leave each other notes to figure out what's going on. They slowly fall in love, but when they try to finally meet in real life, they discover a shocking secret about time and a falling comet.
