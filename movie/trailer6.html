My Neighbor Totoro

Director: Hayao Miyazaki | Studio: Studio Ghibli

Family Slice of Life Spirits Classic

"Magic is all around us if we just look."

Two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, move to an old house in the countryside to be closer to their sick mother's hospital. While exploring outside, they find out the forest is full of friendly, invisible spirits. They meet a giant, fluffy creature they name Totoro. Totoro takes them on magical adventures and helps them stay happy while they worry about their mom.
