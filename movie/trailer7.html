Violet Evergarden

Director: Taichi Ishidate | Studio: Kyoto Animation

Drama Emotional Romance Masterpiece

"Writing letters to learn what 'I love you' means."

Violet is a young girl who was raised only to be a soldier in a terrible war. Now that the war is over, she has metal arms and doesn't know what to do with her life. Her boss's last words to her were "I love you," but she doesn't know what that means. To find out, she gets a job writing letters for other people, learning about human feelings along the way.
