Yūki Tabata

Famous Work: Black Clover

Fantasy Magic Shōnen Action Rising Manga Creator

"Hard work can surpass even the greatest magic."

Yūki Tabata is a Japanese manga artist best known for creating the popular fantasy series Black Clover. The manga started in 2015 in Weekly Shōnen Jump and became successful worldwide. It was later adapted into anime, movies, and games. Tabata is known for his exciting battle scenes and strong character development.
