Gege Akutami

Famous Work: Jujutsu Kaisen

Dark Fantasy Supernatural Action Modern Shōnen Fast-Rising Creator

"Darkness reveals the true power within the soul."

Gege Akutami is a Japanese manga artist born on February 26, 1992 in Iwate Prefecture. Akutami became internationally famous after creating Jujutsu Kaisen in 2018. The series became one of the best-selling modern manga known for its dark fantasy themes and intense fight scenes. Akutami keeps their personal life very private.
